Organoid Intelligence

Hartung defines organoid intelligence as "reproducing cognitive functions, such as learning and sensory processing, in a lab-grown human-brain model."
The brain organoids that Hartung currently uses would need to be scaled up for OI, or organoid intelligence.Organoids are lab-grown tissues that resemble organs.
These three-dimensional structures, usually derived from stem cells, have been used in labs for nearly two decades, where scientists have been able to avoid harmful human
or animal testing by experimenting on the stand-ins for kidneys, lungs and other organs.Read more
